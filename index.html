Our Vision

We main vision is to encourage our business and our beloved students for the startups in our district Madhepura. For that We are here to support them at every level. Every month we motivates and invites some founders from other startups from bihar to guide for students in every possible way. Bihar goverment provides the loan of 10 Lacs for the statup for interest free upto 10 years. In some cases it get extended upto 75 lacs. We are always ready to guide you and help you to build your startup under startup bihar scheme.
